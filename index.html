Stipulated Order Modifying Child Custody and Visitation

FILED: DEC 27, 2024 · JUDGE STEVEN K. SHARPE
Joint Legal Custody — Mother (Tara) retains primary physical custody subject to Father's (Jason's) parenting time:
  • Week 1: Father has child Friday 4:30pm → Monday morning (school drop-off or return to Mother by 4:30pm)
  • Week 2: Father has child Thursday 4:30pm → Friday morning (school drop-off or return to Mother by 4:30pm)
  • Summer & Holidays: Per Amended Standard Visitation Order filed Nov 12, 2013

Communication: All co-parenting communication via Our Family Wizard app only.
Right of First Refusal: If either parent is unavailable for more than 2 hours during their parenting time, they must offer the other parent the opportunity to care for the child.

Amended Standard Visitation Order

FILED: NOV 12, 2013
Alternate Weekends (school year): Non-custodial parent has even-numbered Fridays (6pm) through Sunday (6pm). If Monday is a holiday, through Monday 6pm.

Summer Visitation:
  • Non-custodial parent (Jason) has minimum 2 months, beginning the first Saturday after school ends
  • 60-day period ending at 7pm on day 60
  • Mother has alternate-holiday and alternate-weekend schedule during summer
  • Mother entitled to 1 uninterrupted week in July with 25-day advance notice to Father
Holidays: Alternate by odd/even years per attached schedule.

Agreements

BOTH PARTIES AGREED
  • Summer visitation weekends: Every other weekend during summer belongs to the non-custodial parent — alternating weekends, not tied to odd/even Friday dates. Current weekend of Mar 8–9, 2026 is the non-custodial parent's. Pattern alternates from there through summer.

Child Support

ORDERED NOV 9, 2013
Father (Jason) pays Mother (Tara) $220.16/month, beginning September 15, 2013. Payments via Clerk of District Court, Laramie County. Abates during Jason's summer visitation upon filing of claim.

Medical insurance provided by Tara (Blue Cross Blue Shield). Uncovered costs split 50/50.

Supplemental Order — General Conduct

FILED: NOV 12, 2013
  • Neither parent shall disparage the other in front of the child
  • No excessive drinking or illegal substances in child's presence
  • No violence, immoral conduct, or obscenities in child's presence
  • No interference with child's relationship with the other parent
  • Right of First Refusal applies (2+ hour threshold)
  • Drug & alcohol use while child is in your care = material change of circumstances sufficient to seek custody modification
